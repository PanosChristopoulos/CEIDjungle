Mohamed Salah's record-breaking performances saw him receive a number of other accolades,
including the PFA Players' Player of the Year, the Football Writers' Player
of the Year and the PFA Fans' Player of the Year awards. Salah also came third
in the award for the 2018 Best FIFA Men's Player. The following season he was
joint recipient of the Premier League Golden Boot with 22 goals, and in the 2019
UEFA Champions League Final he opened the scoring as Liverpool won 2–0.
